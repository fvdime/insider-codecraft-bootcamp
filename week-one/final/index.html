Jinx, previously known as Powder, is one of the main characters in Arcane. She is the unpredictable, ingenious, and chaotic little sister of Vi and adoptive daughter to Silco.

Viktor was a main character of Arcane. He came from the Undercity to become an inventor and created Hextech alongside his partner, Jayce Talis.

Jayce

Jayce Talis was a main character in Arcane. He and his mother were saved by arcane magic and he became dedicated to finding a way to harness this magic.
